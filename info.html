Info

Clean water is very important for people's lives. But it is still scarce in many countries. In this section we explain why this is the case and how different areas use water. We also show what can be done to conserve water and improve its quality..

Industry

Industrial plants use a lot of water for their work. Sometimes the water becomes dirty and harmful to nature afterwards. If you use new technologies, you can waste less water and make less waste.

Households

In every home, people use water every day: washing hands, taking showers, cooking, washing clothes. In countries with good infrastructure, water comes directly from the tap, but in many places in the world it must be brought by hand from wells or rivers. This sometimes takes a lot of time and effort for people. If people learn to conserve water and use simple filters, they can improve their quality of life and reduce the risk of disease.
